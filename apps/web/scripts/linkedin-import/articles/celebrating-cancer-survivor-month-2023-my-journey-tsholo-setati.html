Celebrating Cancer Survivor Month 2023: My Journey as a Cancer Survivor

Created on 2023-05-30 15:46

Published on 2023-06-01 05:00

Hey there LinkedIn family! This month is the month when we honour those who've been through the storm and come out dancing in the rain. Yes, it's Cancer Survivorship Month, and I'm here to share a bit of my own journey.

I was diagnosed with cancer last year early March 2022 after a long stay in ICU. It was a right hook left hook combination I did not see coming, no doubt about it. However, I am a survivor actually a sur-thriver...hashtag. I got rocked but I stayed in the fight and I'd like to share a few lessons I've learned along the way. This one's for all the young people out there who've had their lives turned upside down by this beast but for everyone else as well.

  1. Listen, You're Stronger Than You Think: Battling cancer showed me just how resilient I am. It's like discovering you've got a lion's heart in a time of crisis. And you know what? You've got one too.
  2. Treatment and Tribulations: The treatment phase is tough, no two ways about it. I underwent a year of treatment with chemo sessions once a week every week. But it's also where you discover a strength you never knew you had. It's like finding out you've got superpowers, just not the kind you'd find in a Marvel or DC movie.
  3. It's Okay to Feel All the Feels: Some days, I was scared. Some days, I was angry. And some days, I was just done with everything. And that's okay. Your emotions are valid. Feel them, understand them, then let them pass.
  4. Lean on Your Support System: My family and my friends became my rock during this journey. Don't be afraid to ask for help, love, or just a bit of understanding. And it's not just about the big gestures. A text, a call, a joke to lighten the mood – they all add up.
  5. Celebrate Small Victories: Every day is a win. Every smile, every good moment, every bit of progress - they're all worth celebrating. So, don't wait for the big wins to feel joy. Making it to treatment is a win.
  6. Life After Cancer: After the dust settles, you realise that surviving cancer doesn't just mean being disease-free. It means appreciating life in a way you never did before. Every sunrise is a bit more beautiful, every laugh a bit more joyous, and every moment a bit more precious.
  7. Remember, It's Your Journey: Every cancer journey is unique, just like every survivor. Don't compare your journey to anyone else's. Just do your best, and remember, you are valid and enough.

So, as we step into Cancer Survivorship Month, let's celebrate the warriors, remember the fallen, and stand strong for those still in the fight. We're all in this together, and together, we're stronger than cancer.

Are you a cancer survivor, or do you know someone who is? Share your stories, your victories, and your hope in the comments. Let's build a community of strength, resilience, and love right here.
