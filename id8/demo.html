AI-RICE Dimensions

Each use case is scored on 5 key dimensions (1-5 scale):

Reach (20%)

How many people/transactions does this affect annually?

Impact (25%)

What's the annual value created per affected unit?

Confidence (20%)

How confident are we in the estimated impact?

Effort (20%)

How much effort to build/deploy? (5=easy, 1=hard)

Strategic Fit (15%)

Alignment with company priorities & strategy
